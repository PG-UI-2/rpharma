Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How quickly can I receive my order?

We dispatch orders within 24 hours. Most locations receive deliveries within 2-3 business days. Express delivery options are available for urgent requirements.

Are all your products genuine?

Yes, 100%. All medicines are sourced directly from licensed manufacturers and verified through strict quality control processes.

Do I need a prescription for all medicines?

For certain medicines, a valid prescription from a licensed doctor is legally required. Our pharmaceutical team will guide you through the process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets (UPI, Google Pay, WhatsApp Pay), net banking, and bank transfers for your convenience.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive tracking information via email once your order is dispatched. You can monitor delivery status in real-time.

What's your return/refund policy?

We offer a hassle-free refund on unopened products within 7 days. Please contact us for details on specific returns.
